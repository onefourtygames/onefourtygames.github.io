# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last updated:** 01/28/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how data is managed in relation to the use of the mobile video game **Block Crash** (hereinafter, "the App"), developed by **OneFourty Games** (hereinafter, "the Developer").

---

## 1. Data Collection

The Developer **does not collect direct personal data** from users.
The App:

* Does not require registration
* Does not request name, email, or phone number
* Does not access precise GPS location
* Does not allow in-app purchases

However, the App integrates **third-party services** that may automatically collect certain information.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses:

**Google AdMob (Google LLC)**

AdMob is used to show ads within the game. To do this, it may collect:

* Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID / GAID / IDFA)
* App usage data (interaction with ads)
* Diagnostic data (crashes, performance)

This data **does not personally identify the user**, but is considered data under privacy regulations.

More information:
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 3. Purpose of Data Use

Data collected by AdMob may be used to:

* Show relevant ads
* Measure advertising performance
* Improve the technical stability of the App

The Developer does not directly access this individual data.

## 4. Legal Basis (EU Users)

For users in the European Union, data processing is based on:

* User consent (managed via the Google/AdMob consent system)
* Legitimate interest for the technical operation of the App

Users can modify their advertising preferences from their device settings.

## 5. User Rights

Depending on the jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, or other laws), users may have the right to:

* Request access to their data
* Request deletion
* Object to processing
* Limit the use of advertising identifiers

Since the Developer does not store direct personal data, these requests should be directed primarily to Google:
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 6. Children

The App **is not specifically directed at children under 13 years of age**.
